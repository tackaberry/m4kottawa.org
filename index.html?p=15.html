Join M4K

This winter in Ottawa, a number of brave souls will sacrifice their faces – and dignities – as Growers for Mustaches for Kids. Our goal is to use our pushbrooms to raise $25,000 for the Make-A-Wish Foundation of Canada (Eastern Ontario chapter). The Ottawa chapter has been growing since 2005 and has raised over $250,000.
