Your mustache is calling, do you accept the charges?

November 6th, 2008 by admin

Hello dudes with budding ‘staches,

This really is the most precious of times of mustache growth – just starting out, can start to see the long tall ‘stache shadow forming. It signals the real start to the M4K season.

This Friday we will be getting together to check-in, have week 1 photos taken, and jostle one another on the first week of growth. Again, this Friday November November 7 we will be at the Clocktower on Bank St The official start time is 8pm.

Your pledging will be starting up about now. You should be planting the seeds of giving to sow in the upcoming couple weeks. See the Grower’s kit for a couple tips on raising money. Keep track of people wanting receipts on the pledge sheet. I’ll run through a couple more house keeping items tomorrow and in a few emails over the next couple of weeks.

Stay cool.

Stachely,
Brett Tackaberry
